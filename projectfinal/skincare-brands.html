Skincare Brands and Products

There are a range of skincare brands that advertise for these skincare concerns: acne, acne scars, dark spots, uneven texture and much more.
It always comes with the question of which skincare brand or products should I choose?


The College Student Dilemma

As a college student, it is hard to invest in skincare that is great for you. Often there are skincare brands that are in the affordable to high-end. As my target is for college students to young adults, I want to introduce two affordable skincare brands that will not hurt your pocket. These two skincare brands offer affordable skin care products that you can create a whole skincare routine. A skincare routine can consist of the following: cleanser, toner, serum, eye moisturizer, and face moisturizer.


Skincare Brands

The brands are the INKEY List and The Ordinary. These are brands that I have previously tried and liked.
